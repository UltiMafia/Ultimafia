Ultimafia — Free Online Mafia & Social Deduction Games

Ultimafia is a free, browser-based social deduction game platform and the spiritual successor to EpicMafia. Play real-time multiplayer games of Mafia, Werewolf, Secret Hitler, The Resistance, Texas Hold'em and more against real people in your browser — no download, no paywall.

What is Ultimafia?

Ultimafia is an online social deduction game site where players are secretly assigned roles on opposing teams (for example, Town vs. Mafia or Village vs. Werewolves) and must use discussion, voting, and deception to identify their enemies before being eliminated.

A free alternative to Town of Salem, mafia.gg and other online mafia games

Ultimafia is a free, browser-based alternative to Town of Salem, mafia.gg, Werewolf Online, Blood on the Clocktower Online, and the original EpicMafia. How to play

Create a free account, join the lobby, pick a game setup, and wait for other players to fill the seats. When the game starts you will be secretly assigned a role and an alignment. Use the day phase to discuss and vote; use the night phase to act on your role's abilities. The game ends when one alignment achieves its win condition.

Why Ultimafia?

Ultimafia was built as a successor to EpicMafia for players who want a free, open, community-driven social deduction site with deep customization. It is open source, actively maintained, and moderated by volunteers. The game engine supports complex role interactions, modular abilities via "cards", items, effects, and priority-ordered action resolution — making it one of the most mechanically rich social deduction platforms available online.
